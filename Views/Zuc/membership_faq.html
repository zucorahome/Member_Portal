About my rewards

How do I earn reward points?

Every $1 dollar spent on your monthly Smarter Living Plan subscription or from shopping earns 10 Smarter Living Reward Points.

How do I see my point balance?

Your account balance is available in your Member Centre Profile, in the Smarter Living Rewards section of the Member Centre, and on the check out shopping screen as long as you’ve logged in to shop.

What is the redemption process?

Smarter Living Reward Points can be redeemed at checkout or you can convert them into an e-card within the Rewards section of the Member Centre.

What can reward points be redeemed for?

Rewards points can be spent on Smarter Home Products or converted into e-gift cards.

Plan details

Are there any exclusions to my service plan or limits to my coverage?

Although the coverage available through each Smarter Living Plan is extensive, there are some exclusions. Refer to the terms & conditions to see a specific list of limitations and items that are excluded from the appliances, furniture, and home-systems coverage available.

I have a mix of new and old furniture, appliances and home systems. Does this matter or will only some items be covered?

The Smarter Living Plan covers your new and existing furniture, appliances and home systems.

Is there a limit to the number of service calls or items that can be replaced within a year?

There is not a limit for the number of service requests, only a limit to the total cost allotment for services or replacements within a 12 month period. See terms & conditions for full details.

Are all Smarter Living Plans available all across Canada?

Smarter Living Furniture and Appliances Plans are available across the country. Smarter Living Home Systems Plans will be available across Canada in 2020.

Can I purchase a Smarter Living Plan for my home in the United States?

Not today, but we are working to provide service in the United States.

Can I upgrade my Smarter Living Plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time.

General Questions

How long has ZucoraHome been in business?

Since 1979, we’ve been helping reduce the challenges of home management with service programs and products designed to deliver peace-of-mind for today’s busy families.

What products and services does ZucoraHome offer?

Our solutions include smarter living plans (to protect and service your furniture, appliances and home systems) together with the latest technology for digitally connected homes. Our smarter living products help maintain clean homes and healthy families.

Where can I buy Zucora Home products?

Almost all of our products can be purchased online at zucorahome.com or from leading retailers.

Are ZucoraHome’s products available outside of Canada?

ZucoraHome’s products and services are available in many areas throughout Canada. Soon, we’ll be able to offer our products and services in other areas around the world.

I have a mix of new and old furniture, appliances and home systems. Does this matter or will only some items be covered?

The Smarter Living Plan covers your new and existing furniture, appliances and home systems. However, existing furniture items would be replaced at a set rate in the form of a gift certificate with a partner retailer.

How do replacements work?

Where Covered Items may require replacement, Zucora Home will issue a gift card with a retail partner for the authorized replacement value.

What is the waiting period between the start of my plan and when I can request service?

Your initial service period begins on the thirtieth (30th) day following the purchase of your Smarter Living Plan.

Is there a limit to the number of service calls or items that can be replaced within a year?

There is not a limit for the number of service requests, only a limit to the total cost allotment for services or replacements per year. See Terms and Conditions for full details.
